## Hi, I'm Breno 👋

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, and author working at the intersection of AI, Bitcoin, and quantitative finance.

My main home on the web is **[brenorb.com](https://brenorb.com)**, where I keep a living archive of my projects, articles, books, talks, podcasts, interviews, and media appearances.

I like building systems that make hard technical ideas easier to use: LLM tools for Bitcoin developers, privacy analysis for wallets, educational graphs, and practical AI products.

### Focus

- AI and LLMs: agents, RAG, evaluation, alignment, and applied ML
- Bitcoin: education, privacy, and open protocols
- Quant finance: options, ergodicity, and research

### Selected Work

- **[101 Perguntas sobre Bitcoin](https://bitcoin101.site)** - my book for learning Bitcoin through clear questions and answers
- **[BIP39 Portuguese Wordlist](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0039/portuguese.txt)** - co-created the official Portuguese wordlist for Bitcoin wallet backups
- **[Stealth](https://github.com/stealth-bitcoin/stealth)** - Bitcoin wallet privacy auditing from public descriptors
- **[btc-graph](https://brenorb.com/btc-graph/)** - interactive Bitcoin learning graph with curated prerequisites and resources
- **Minimaxis** - SatsHack-winning AI assistant for Bitcoin Miniscript
- **Stringer Safety** - 1st-place HRF AI Hack for Freedom project for journalist safety workflows
